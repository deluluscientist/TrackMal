# TrackML (v1.0)
### Smart Price Monitor & Personal Finance Manager

**TrackML** is a powerful, lightweight mobile utility designed to help you master your money. By combining real-time price monitoring with an intuitive expense manager, TrackML ensures you never overpay and always stay within your budget.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Smart Price Monitor:** Stop checking websites manually. Track price fluctuations on your favorite items and receive instant alerts when prices drop.
* **Expense Tracker:** Log daily income and expenses in seconds. Categorize your spending to understand your financial habits.
* **Financial Insights:** Visualize your data through clean, easy-to-read charts and monthly spending summaries.
* **Budget Planning:** Set monthly limits for different categories (e.g., Food, Transport) and get notified as you approach your ceiling.
* **Optimized Performance:** Built for speed and efficiency, ensuring a smooth experience even on entry-level devices.

---

## 🛡️ Privacy & Security

We believe financial data is the most personal data. TrackML is built with a **Privacy-First** philosophy:

* **Local Data Storage:** Your sensitive financial records are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal transaction logs to our servers.
* **End-to-End Encryption:** Any data processed for price alerts is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols.
* **Zero-Tracking:** We do not track, profile, or sell your spending habits. Your data stays yours.

## 📥 Installation Guide (Android)

As a specialized release, you can install the app manually using the provided APK.

1.  **Download:** Get the `app-release.apk` from the [Official Download Folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1UQ317Y_TljyUHU8jOeyZQlMYvMnsJKWP?usp=drive_link).
2.  **Enable Unknown Sources:** Navigate to your phone's **Settings > Security** (or Apps) and toggle on **"Install from Unknown Sources"**.
3.  **Install:** Open the downloaded APK file and tap **Install**.
4.  **Launch:** Find the TrackML icon on your home screen and start your journey toward financial freedom.
